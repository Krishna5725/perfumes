What Are Perfume Types?

Perfumes are classified into different types based on their fragrance composition. Each type has its own unique blend of ingredients that creates a specific scent. Discover the most common types of perfumes and their characteristics.

Popular Perfume Types

Floral

Floral perfumes are made from the scent of flowers. They are light, fresh, and romantic.

Woody

Woody perfumes have a warm, earthy scent. They are deep, rich, and perfect for evening wear.

Oriental

Oriental perfumes are exotic and spicy, often combining warm ingredients like vanilla, amber, and musk.
